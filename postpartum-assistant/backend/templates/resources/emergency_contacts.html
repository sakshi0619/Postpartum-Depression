When to Seek Help

Contact a healthcare provider immediately if you experience:

  • Thoughts of harming yourself or your baby
  • Severe anxiety or panic attacks
  • Inability to sleep even when baby is sleeping
  • Feelings of hopelessness or despair
  • Loss of interest in activities you used to enjoy

Emergency Plan

Create your postpartum emergency plan:

  1. Identify trusted people to call
  2. Save important numbers in your phone
  3. Share your plan with your support system
  4. Know the nearest emergency facilities
