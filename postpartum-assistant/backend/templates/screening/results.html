Interpretation of Your Results

A score of 15 on the Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale indicates a moderate likelihood of experiencing depressive symptoms. While this screening tool does not provide a diagnosis, scores of 13 or above suggest that you may benefit from further evaluation by a healthcare professional.

It's important to understand that the EPDS is specifically designed to identify possible depression in postpartum individuals, but can also be used as a general depression screening tool. Clinical Recommendations

Based on your EPDS score of 15, we recommend the following:

  • Consult a healthcare professional such as your primary care physician, obstetrician, or a mental health specialist for a comprehensive evaluation.
  • Consider sharing these results with your healthcare provider to facilitate discussion about your mental health.

Important Disclaimer

This screening tool is not a diagnostic instrument. It is designed to assist in identifying symptoms that may indicate depression and to facilitate discussion with a healthcare professional. Only a qualified healthcare provider can make a diagnosis of depression or other mental health conditions. If you are in crisis or having thoughts of harming yourself or others, please seek immediate professional help.
